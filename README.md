# **Limericks**

Pig
-
There once was a big pig,

 Who went to a gig,
 
 He ate a chair,
 
 Next to a pear,
 
 That looked like a fig.

Lady
-
There once was a young girl called Sadie,

Who met a stinky old lady,

The lady looked a bit blue,

She said she need the loo,

This girl who met a stinky old lady.
